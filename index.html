What is Fútbol?

Fútbol is the king of sports, it is a team sport played between two teams of eleven players each, while the referees ensure that the rules are followed correctly, this sport is practiced all over the world and has many names, in Spain: Fútbol (or Furbo), in the UK: Football, in the USA: Soccer, in Norway: Fotball, in Germany: Fußball, in Italy: Calcio. This sport is one of the oldest in history and has been changing throughout the ages until today, there are indications of similar games for more than 2000 years, in fact the oldest officially recognized form of this sport dates back to Ancient Greece.
