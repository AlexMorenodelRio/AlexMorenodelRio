Here we will see the most important rules.

It is played with a ball.

The matches last 90 minutes divided into two 45-minute parts and a 15-minute break.

They play on a field of minimum 90m and maximum 110m long, and 90m maximum and 45m minimum for width.

The field is made up of two goals, and two areas, one on each shorter side of the rectangle and a central circle in the center of the field.

Each team will play with 11 starting players, one of them being the goalkeeper and they will have 5 substitutions.

There are two types of cards that the referee can show the players, the yellow card is a warning to the player and the second yellow card the player will be expelled, and the red card that is direct expulsion.

The goalkeepers will be able to touch the ball with their hands or arms inside their area, while the other players will not be able to touch the ball with their arms or hands.

Each team must wear a color that is easily distinguished from the rival's color and the goalkeepers will wear different colors from the teams for their distinction.
